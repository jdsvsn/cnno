# Project Overview
The CNNO landing page project aims to create a visually stunning and user-friendly online presence for the Cinnamon roll business. The primary objective is to showcase the products and services offered by CNNO, providing an elegant and seamless experience for customers to explore and engage with the brand.

## Target Audience
The target audience for the CNNO landing page includes:
* Demographics: Foodies, individuals with a sweet tooth, and those looking for a unique dessert experience
* Psychographics: People seeking high-quality, artisanal cinnamon rolls and a welcoming brand atmosphere
* User personas:
	+ Young adults (18-35 years old) who frequently try new food trends
	+ Families with young children who enjoy baked goods and desserts
	+ Individuals with a strong online presence, active on social media platforms

## Functional Requirements
The following functional requirements are necessary for the CNNO landing page:
* Display a hero section showcasing a high-quality image of a cinnamon roll
* Include a brief introduction to the business and its mission
* Feature a section highlighting the different types of cinnamon rolls offered
* Provide a call-to-action (CTA) for users to order online or visit a physical location
* Implement a newsletter signup form for users to stay updated on promotions and new products
* Ensure responsive design for a seamless user experience across various devices

## Design Guidelines
The design of the CNNO landing page will follow an elegant style, incorporating the brand's primary colors:
* Primary color: #3b82f6 (a soft, calming blue)
* Secondary color: #1e293b (a deep, rich gray-blue)
* Typography: Open Sans, with font sizes and line heights adjusted for optimal readability
* Imagery: High-quality images of cinnamon rolls, with a mix of close-up shots and lifestyle photos
* Layout: Clean and minimalistic, with ample white space to create a sense of sophistication

## Tech Stack
The CNNO landing page will be built using the following technologies:
* Frontend framework: Next.js 14
* CSS framework: Tailwind
* Programming language: TypeScript
* Deployment: Vercel or a similar platform for optimal performance and scalability

## Future Roadmap
Future developments and iterations for the CNNO landing page include:
* Integrating e-commerce functionality for online orders and payment processing
* Implementing a customer review and rating system
* Creating a blog section for sharing recipes, behind-the-scenes content, and company news
* Expanding the newsletter to include exclusive promotions and limited-time offers
* Conducting A/B testing and user experience research to inform design and functionality improvements
